Why being a Buddy is important?

A new employee in the first initial days of joining tends to be hesitant and at times confused as he/she may not know what is the accepted norm, job role, processes, or culture.

This buddy program will accelerate the new employee's ability to deal with these early disconcerting issues by attaching new employees with employees who have been with the company for some time and who not only understand but also can explain the process, system, job role, interpersonal connect within the team, as well as introduce the new employee to the key people in the function.
